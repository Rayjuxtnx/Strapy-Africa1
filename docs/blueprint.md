# **App Name**: Curated Finds

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display a hero section with brand info, featured collections, popular products, and direct CTAs.
- Product Listing: Categorize and list products with images, names, prices, and 'Add to Cart' buttons.
- Detailed Product View: Offer detailed product information, including multiple images, descriptions, and size/color options.
- Shopping Cart Management: Allow users to view, modify quantities, and remove items from their cart.
- Streamlined Checkout: Implement a simple checkout process with essential fields (name, email, address) and payment options.
- Customer Service Chat: An AI chatbot is trained on FAQs. The AI will answer user's question and determine whether or not it can offer information on that query. If the AI tool cannot handle the request, it escalates to a human customer support representative.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep violet (#673AB7), for a stylish and inviting feel.
- Background color: Very light purple (#F3E5F5).
- Accent color: Royal blue (#3F51B5).
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif with a high-end feel; body font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif to make body copy highly readable.
- Minimalist icons for categories and cart interactions.
- Clean, responsive design with a focus on product imagery.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to enhance user experience.
